my projects

Artemis

Artemis Studios (formerly 14 Studios) is a Discord-based development studio, formed by developers from all over the world. The studio creates applications and services for communities and the general public. I used to lead the studio along with three other great people, but I left the studio in November of 2020. I re-joined Artemis in January of 2021, and I spearhead DevOps (along with being an owner) on the team.

Delta

Delta Development is a development studio that offers a Discord bot creation and bot hosting service, besides the two bots that we run. We create custom bots there and host some other stuff. It's pretty cool, you should check it out!

Lights On Studios

Lights On Studios is a development studio. We create Discord bots for many purposes, in addition to offering other kinds of stuff. Two other fine gentlemen and myself lead the studio. We do some pretty cool work there.

Squared Labs

Squared Labs is a development studio. It's lead by myself and a few other people. We develop Vision Browser and a few other projects. We also create some fun tools for people to mess around with.

Kiwi

Kiwi was an Artemis project that I worked on alongside 5 other great people. It was a multi-purpose Discord bot focused on utilities and moderation. The project dissolved in December 2020, with Artemis having restructured in order to become a Minecraft server in January 2021.

Vision Browser

A simple web browser created in Electron. It was discontinued back in March/April of 2020, but I resurrected in December of 2020. It's now a project of Squared Labs.

BioRPC

A simple wrapper for discord.bio.

Unicord

Formerly my Discord bot. I ended up closing shop for Unicord in August 2020 after I became tired of the project.
