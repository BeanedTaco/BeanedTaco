About me

I'm a web designer, full-stack developer, VFX editor, and a 3D modeler. I create things both for fun and for business. I run multiple projects. 

As a full-stack developer, I mainly dedicate my time to creating bots for the chat platform Discord. I also do web design.

In my free-time, I moderate and administrate multiple Discord servers. 


If I'm not working, schooling, or moderating, I play video games. I also watch anime (#bungoustraydogs). Other than that, I'm very very tech savvy, and I love messing with technology.

Contact me

You can contact me on Discord, or you can email me at danny@bean.codes.
