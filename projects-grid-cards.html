Squared Labs

Squared Labs is a development studio. It's lead by myself and a few other people. We develop Vision Browser and a few other projects. We also create some fun tools for people to mess around with.

Artemis

Artemis Studios (formerly 14 Studios) is a Discord-based development studio, formed by developers from all over the world. The studio creates applications and services for communities and the general public. I used to lead the studio along with three other great people, but I left the studio in December of 2020.

Kiwi

Kiwi is a Artemis project that I'm working on alongside 5 other great people. It's a multi-purpose Discord bot focused on utilities and moderation.

Vision Browser

A simple web browser created in Electron. It was discontinued back in March/April of 2020, but I resurrected in December of 2020. It's now a project of Squared Labs.
